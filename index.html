Naomi

Ph.D. Candidate in Marketing

HKU Business School, The University of Hong Kong

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Marketing at HKU Business School, The University of Hong Kong. My research interests center on gift-giving, behavioral pricing, and choice structure, with a particular focus on digital marketing and prosocial behavior. I utilize a range of methodological approaches, including lab, online, and field experiments, platform A/B tests, eye-tracking, and analysis of longitudinal and secondary data.
